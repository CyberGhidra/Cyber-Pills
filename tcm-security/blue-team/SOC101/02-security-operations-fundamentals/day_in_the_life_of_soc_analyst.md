# Day in the Life of a SOC Analyst

## Rotating Schedules
- Analysts may work in rotating shifts (24/7 operations)
- Ensures continuous monitoring and coverage

## Alert Triage
- Monitor and manage the alert ticket queue
- Assess alert credibility, severity, and urgency

## Incident Triage
- Gather initial information
- Follow established triage and escalation processes

## First-Line Analysis and Investigation
- Use tools and techniques to assess incidents
- Review logs, network traffic, configurations
- Leverage OSINT when needed

## Documentation and Reporting
- Clear and concise reporting
- Document findings, actions taken, and resolutions

## Escalation and Collaboration
- Coordinate with internal teams
- Escalate incidents to higher-tier analysts when required

## Continuous Improvement and Cross-Training
- Participate in training to improve skill sets
- Enhance detection and response effectiveness
